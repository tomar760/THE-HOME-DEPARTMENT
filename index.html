Frequently Asked Questions

How to file an online complaint?

Go to the Services section and click on "Online FIR Filing".

Is payment required for any services?

Most services are free. Nominal charges apply for premium reports.

How can I report a cybercrime?

You can report cybercrime through our "Online FIR Filing" service or contact our helpline.

About Us

The Home Department is dedicated to ensuring internal security, maintaining law and order, and fostering a safe environment for all citizens. Our mission is to protect lives, safeguard property, and uphold justice through proactive measures and rapid response.

We work tirelessly to enhance public safety, manage emergencies effectively, and combat new age threats like cybercrime using advanced technology and a highly trained workforce.

Our Mission

To secure the nation by fostering peace, preventing crime, and responding effectively to all forms of emergencies.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and efficient public safety agency, safeguarding the future of our citizens.
