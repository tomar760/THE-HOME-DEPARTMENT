Refund Policy

We follow a transparent and fair refund policy for all transactions made through our website.

Eligibility for Refund

  • If payment was made by mistake
  • If service was not delivered as promised

Time Frame

All refund requests must be raised within 7 working days of transaction.

Procedure

To request a refund, email us at support@homedept.in with your transaction details and reason.

Exceptions

Refunds will not be processed for voluntary donations or successfully delivered services.

Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 working days back to the original payment method.
